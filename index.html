Tips & Notes

  • Waymark Variants: Use the toggle in the top-left to switch between BP, ABBA, MR, and X.
  • Pattern Recognition: The first pair of ice and blue circles defines the pattern.
  • Positions: Numbers/letters correspond to waymark positions on the platform.
  • X waymarks: This corresponds to no waymarks and uses the following codes Stay, Cross, Left and Right (looking inwards).
